BEST TOURIST SPOTS IN THE PHILIPPINES

Fort Santiago Intramuros, Manila This is one of the top landmarks in Manila, was erected by Migel de Legazpi and by the end of World War II was destroyed
Kawasan Falls, Cebu It is a multi-layered waterfall in the town of Badian, Cebu and is known for its very smooth and clear waters
Kayangan Lake, Coron Palawan If you want to take a dip in a very clean, clear, beautiful and amazing tourist spot when you are visiting Philippines, this is your go to place.
Rizal Park, Manila Rizal Park is one of the oldest parks in Manila. It is also very memorable as the death place of Jose Rizal, the "pambansang bayani" of the Philippines getting its name, Rizal Park.
